Clearpath



For my fourth and most recent co-op term, I worked at Clearpath, formerly known as Clearpath Robotics. Clearpath is a robotics company that focuses on autonomous navigation in industrial application as well as robotics for research platforms.

At Clearpath I was a member of the Test and Infrastructure team, as the Test Engineering Analyst Co-op. My tasks were essentially to streamline or eliminate some of the more mundane or menial tasks of the test team.



My most significant project was a robot fleet monitoring system. I designed a Django application that would poll a fleet management system and report back on it by notifying selected users of failures.

I also worked on different extensions to the open source software Sentry. Sentry is a crash reporting system for Linux. I worked on extensions that would improve the notification system as well as the organization of the crash reports. Other tasks included log parsing and log generating.
